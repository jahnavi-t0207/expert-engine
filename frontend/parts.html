Engine Components

Piston

Moves up and down converting combustion energy into motion.

Crankshaft

Converts piston movement into rotational motion.

Camshaft

Controls opening and closing of engine valves.

Valves

Allow air fuel mixture in and exhaust gases out.

Spark Plug

Ignites air fuel mixture inside the cylinder.

Cylinder Block

Main engine body containing cylinders and pistons.

Timing Belt

Synchronizes crankshaft and camshaft movement.

Oil Pump

Circulates oil to lubricate engine components.
